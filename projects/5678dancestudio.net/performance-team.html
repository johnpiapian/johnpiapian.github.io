RHYTHM! DANCERS

Performance Team

Rhythm! Dancers is our performance team here at 5678 Dance! Studio. The group is comprised of older students ages 12 - 18 that are chosen by ability and commitment to dancing.

Our goal with this group is to give them more performance opportunities other than our annual recital. Our performances have included Six Flags Great America, Milwaukee Bucks Games, Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin State Fair, local senior centers and nursing homes, the OAC open house and the Oconomowoc Festival of Arts. Rhythm! also takes a trip every other year to New York City. Our dancers get the chance to take classes with some of our country's best teachers. They see a Broadway show, the Statue of Liberty and experience many other sights of the city that never sleeps!

These dancers take a minimum of 2 classes per week plus an additional rehearsal class. We have a 30-minute show including ballet, theatrical, and jazz dances. If you are interested in more information on becoming a Rhythm! Dancer please email Miss Michelle at 5678danceoconomowoc@gmail.com
