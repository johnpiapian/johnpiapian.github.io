DANCE STYLES WE LOVE TO TEACH...

DANCE CLASSES are divided by age and experience. Please email us at 5678danceoconomowoc@gmail.com for class time options specific to your child.
NOTE: Current students will have recommendations marked on their brochure which was mailed out on July 31st.

CLASSICAL BALLET

is the most graceful and discipline style of dance. Ballet positions and vocabulary are taught starting at the beginning level. Each class will learn proper classical ballet movement through various barre exercises, stretches, jumps, and combinations. As the class gets more advanced they will be introduced to ballet technique that will improve their placement, alignment, coordination and musicality. Ballet classes are offered for the beginner to advanced student, ages 3 & up.

TAP

is an intricate style of dance where rhythmic sounds are created by the feet. This class is full of upbeat music and quick movements. Tap classes are offered for the beginner to advanced student, ages 4 & up.

POM & DANCE

is one of our most popular classes! Students learn various turns, jumps, kicklines, and arm positions. Highly energetic routines are taught & Poms are used during each class. Pom & Dance classes are offered for the beginner to intermediate student, ages 5 &up.

JAZZ

is a free style form of dance that emphasizes precision, freedom, and high intensity of movement. Class always begins with a series of exercises to build flexibility, strengthen, and avoid possible injury. Very much like ballet, jazz tends to be very technical. This is an up beat class that uses popular music and has faster movements. Jazz classes are offered for the beginner to advanced student, ages 7 & up.

THEATRICAL DANCE

is the class for you if you think it would be fun to learn the style of dance performed in musical productions. This class will give you a chance to learn Broadway style dance choreography and sing at the same time. Theatrical dance classes are offered for students of all levels, ages 8 & up.

HIP HOP

is a blend of today's high energy "street style" movements. This style of dance is a fun, high energy class full of smooth and sharp movements. Hip Hop is the latest dance phenomenon and is offered for the beginner to advanced student, ages 8 & up

LYRICAL BALLET

is a fluid style of dance that combines the basic steps of classical ballet with more free-flowing movement. Dances are taught using the lyrics from the song. Lyrical ballet classes are offered to students ages 12 & up.

CONTEMPORARY

is a style of expressive dance that combines elements of several dance genres including modern, jazz, lyrical and classical ballet. Contemporary dancers strive to connect the mind and the body through fluid dance movements. Classes are offered for dancers ages 12 & up.
